About Rox Engineering

Building Kenya's Future with Precision and Integrity

Rox Engineering Ltd is a top-tier civil and structural engineering, environmental, health and safety consultancy firm based in Kenya. We deliver high-impact, regulation-aligned engineering solutions that combine innovation, quality, and client-centered delivery.

From luxury residential and commercial developments to major infrastructure, industrial, water and sanitation, energy, institutional, and e-mobility projects, we deliver with precision and integrity.

Our multidisciplinary team brings together licensed engineers, environmental consultants, and certified safety professionals to deliver integrated solutions across the full project lifecycle. We work seamlessly across every phase"”from initial concept and design through construction supervision and final handover"”ensuring that every project meets the highest standards of quality, compliance, and excellence.

Eng. James Karori

Director & Founder

With over 9 years of hands-on experience in civil and structural engineering, Eng. James Karori leads Rox Engineering with a commitment to innovation, quality, and regulatory excellence. His expertise spans the full spectrum of engineering disciplines"”from structural design and construction supervision to environmental impact assessments and safety compliance.

Under his leadership, Rox Engineering has grown to become one of Kenya's most trusted engineering consultancies, having successfully delivered over 500 projects across residential, commercial, infrastructure, industrial, water, energy, and institutional sectors.
